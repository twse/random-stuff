The font company [MacCampus](http://www.maccampus.de) originally sold a set of commercial non-Unicode fonts (Helvetica and Times) for Windows and macOS for the Basque language.

Times New Roman Basque (2001), by Libor Sztemon, is a font derived from Times New Roman and includes letters for Basque, Esperanto and Maltese. The PCL5 data refers to the original Times New Roman font. Download the font [from here](http://ls78.sweb.cz/fonts.htm).

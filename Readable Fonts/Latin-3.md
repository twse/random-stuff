## Fonts and keyboards
Esperanta Klavaro (1999-2010), by Yury Finkel under the GPL 2.0 license, is a utility to replace an unused codepage. Go to the [Esperanta Klavaro page](http://www.esperanto.mv.ru/Ek/), in Esperanto only / nur en Esperanto. Ignore the interstitial ads in this website.

The DOS font collection by Joseph Gil includes a font for Esperanto characters in non-standard encoding.

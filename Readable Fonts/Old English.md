## Fonts and keyboards
Lexa and LinguaFont (1998), by Raymond Hickey, includes a built-in font for Old English and a keyboard driver for Helsinki.

Washington Romanized Indic (2011), originally by Thomas Ridgeway and maintained by Anshuman Pandey, contains a font for Old English. The encoding includes insular letterforms and is suitable for typesetting Icelandic and Faroese texts.
